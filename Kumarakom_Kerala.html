Kumarakom.

About.

Kumarakom is a beautiful village located in the Kottayam district of the state of Kerala, India. Situated on the picturesque Vembanad Lake, which is the largest lake in Kerala, Kumarakom is renowned for its stunning backwaters, lush greenery, and tranquil ambiance. It is a popular tourist destination and offers a unique opportunity to experience the enchanting beauty of Kerala's backwaters. Backwaters, Kumarakom is famous for its backwater tourism. The Vembanad Lake, with its interconnected canals and lagoons, forms a mesmerizing network of backwaters. Tourists can take a houseboat or traditional "kettuvallam" cruise through these serene waters, passing by coconut groves, paddy fields, and rural villages, offering a glimpse into the traditional Kerala lifestyl Bird Sanctuary, Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary, also known as Vembanad Bird Sanctuary, is a significant attraction for nature enthusiasts and bird watchers. Spread over 14 acres, the sanctuary is home to a variety of migratory and resident bird species, including herons, egrets, kingfishers, darters, and more. The best time to visit is during the migratory season (November to February) when several bird species from different parts of the world visit the sanctuar Houseboat Stays, Spending a night on a houseboat in the backwaters is a must-do experience in Kumarakom. These houseboats are well-equipped with modern amenities and offer a chance to relax and enjoy the scenic beauty while floating on the tranquil water Ayurvedic Spa and Wellness, Kumarakom offers excellent Ayurvedic spa and wellness centers where visitors can indulge in rejuvenating and traditional Ayurvedic treatments. These therapies are known for their healing and relaxing effect Pathiramanal Island, Situated in Vembanad Lake, Pathiramanal Island is a small, picturesque island that can be accessed by boat. The island is a haven for birdwatchers and nature lovers, offering an opportunity to spot various bird species and enjoy the natural beaut Kumarakom Beach, Though not as popular as the backwaters, Kumarakom also has a serene and clean beach where visitors can enjoy a peaceful stroll along the shore or watch the beautiful sunse Traditional Cuisine, Kumarakom offers a delicious array of traditional Kerala cuisine. Don't miss trying some local delicacies, especially the freshly caught seafood dishes. Kumarakom's laid-back charm, lush landscapes, and the opportunity to experience the unique ecosystem of Kerala's backwaters make it an ideal destination for those seeking a peaceful and rejuvenating vacation amid nature's splendor. It is also a perfect place to get a taste of Kerala's rich cultural heritage and warm hospitality.
